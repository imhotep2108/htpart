\begin{theorem}
    Let \(\symbfit{A}\in K^{n\times n}\), where \(K\) is an algebraically closed field. Then \(\symbfit{A}\) is, and is uniquely up to order of the Jordan blocks, similar to a direct sum of Jordan blocks \(\bigoplus_{i=1}^r\symbfit{J}_i=\symbfit{J}\), which is called the \coloremph{Jordan canonical form} of \(\symbfit{A}\).
\end{theorem}
